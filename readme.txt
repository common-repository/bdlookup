Not sure whether it is appropriate to use a specific word in your blog. This plugin from BeeDictionary.com identifies difficult and confusing words and gives a small popup on hovering.

== Description ==

Quick Dictionary Meaning from BeeDictionary.com creates a small temporary popup for difficult and confusing words. A reader can check the meaning without opening a dictonary or going to other sites.

It has a database of more than 3000 difficult and commonly confused errors. It identifies those words and create a small popup on hovering these words.

== Installation ==

1. Upload the plugin package to your wp-content/plugins directory
1. Unzip the package (unzip bdlookup.zip)
1. Go to your "plugins" administration page and activate the Quick English Dictionary Meaning plugin


== Frequently Asked Questions ==

= How many words are there in the list and what is the criterion for selecting these words =

There are more than 3000 words which this plugin takes care of. These are taken from analysis of more than 2000 open source books. We have taken a frequency list all the words and put it to our manual screening process. What came out is the list of words.

= Some of the words are very easy and should not have been there? =

True. This is called sampling error in statistics. We will fix this in next version.

= My word is not there in the list. =

Send us the word and we will try to include it in our next release.
